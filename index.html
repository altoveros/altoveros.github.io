About

Hi there! I am a Software Engineer specializing in IT, and a Video Game Developer based in San Diego. You can check some of my work here!

I graduated from California State University San Marcos with a B.S in Computer Science in 2021. I enjoy learning about new technology, especially information about applications and video games. I would love to develop and create new products that could be potentially used by others.

Work

Curbie

Inspired by both Mario and Kirby, this is a 2D platformer game where you play as Kirby going through a level to defeat some ghostly monsters.

This was made back in 2016 as a final project in HS, and was developed in Java. I worked as one of the two programmers of the team as well as one of the sprite creators.

If you would like to try it download it below! You will need Java installed on your machine to run this.

Instructions

Movement: WASD (W to Jump)

Goal: Destroy enemies by jumping ontop of them!

Starfield Escape

The year is 2200, Humans have continued to pollute the earth making it uninhabitable. Only a couple hundred have managed to escape via space travel in a search of a new home. You are the captain of the spaceship, hearing a loud crash you realize your ship is being hijacked by aliens. Defeat the leader of the aliens to save the last of the human race!

Starfield Escape was inspired by the likes of Pokemon Mystery Dungeon and Roguelites, this is a 3D Bird's eye view shooter where your goal is to escape randomly generated levels on an abandoned ship with several monsters on board. Every move you make an enemy will move at the same time, so tread carefully!

This game was created back in 2021 with a team of 5 developed in C# and Unity. I worked as one of the programmers of the team, primarily focusing on gameplay mechanics, enemy AI, core systems, and design within Unity.

If you would like to try it download it below!

Instructions

Movement: WASD

Shoot: Spacebar

Goal: Destroy enemies by shooting them and finding the escape portal!

Pawsibitilies

Pawsibilties is an application that is heavily inspired by Pokemon and its PokeDex. This application aims to show potential pet owners what kinds of dog breeds there are and it's different characteristics.

This application was created back in 2020 with a team of 5 developed in Android Studio using the language Dart. I worked as one of the front and back-end programmers for this application as it uses Google Firebase for its authentication. Users were able to search various dog breeds and their ideal personalities and fur type.

LED Matrix 32x64

A fun little project I made for a group of friends. I collaborated with a great artist to create a GIF of what they wanted.

This was done by getting an Arduino Mega board and a 32x64 LED Matrix, this project was done in Java. I took each frame of the gif and converted each image to a BitMap and played it in succession. To see the poster board in action see below!
